# You screen — LOCKED spec (approved)

The engineer's self-service hub. Preview: `you-screen-preview.html`.

## Contents (all carried over from existing tiles/pages)
- **Profile** — name, role, assigned van (GET /auth/me).
- **Stat tiles** — Holiday days left (+ Book time off → existing holiday screen,
  unchanged) and Hrs this week (+ Timesheet → engineer-timesheet).
- **My kit & access** — Plant & equipment held, Keys signed to me, Van check,
  Availability & on-call rota.
- **My records** — My Documents (GDPR + staff docs), Holiday, My timesheet.
- **Settings** — Notifications, Personalise (theme + background), Password &
  device, Help & support.
- **Sign out.**

## Explicitly NOT included
- **Certifications / tickets** (Gas Safe, IPAF, 18th Edition) — does not exist
  today and was deferred; the screen ships without it.
