⚙️ Timesheet settings

Tick Travel for anyone who has 30 minutes each way deducted, and Lunch for anyone who has the lunch break deducted (on days over the threshold). Fuel is the per-person switch for whether they get paid mileage — tick it for the self-employed engineers who claim fuel, and set their pence per mile. Rates are used on self-employed invoices. Blank number boxes fall back to the defaults / the person's profile.

🧾 Invoices

Every invoice generated by a self-employed engineer, newest first. Deleting an invoice unlocks that week and frees its number (their next invoice re-uses it).
